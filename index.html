Mouse on Blackboard

This was a final assignment for my After Effects class in college to make a 10 second animation with stroke and crack effects.

So I used two of my personal logos to make an animation similar to the one at the beginning. First, I drew the letter logos on the blackboard with relatively mild looking chalk, and then I used a machine gun to shoot a bunch of violent bullets to write the rectangular logos.

Learning After Effects was really fun for me, and I think it was really worth it.

KUA Sushi

This is a little project I made with my college classmates, it's a little game with a sushi theme. You have to prepare the cost and budget of the sushi, serve the customers during the weekdays, finalize the bill and wait for the next day, and the difficulty will gets more higher.

Because it was too late, we ended up with a work with a lot of bugs.

It's a pity, but it's really worthwhile to work like this, and I learned a lot.

Ravencrofts Family

These six rendered wallpapers are of a Demon family of six, one of whom goes by the name of Nerissa Ravencroft, a Hololive vtuber. Nerissa had previously released a simple drawing of her family, which was eventually completed by Twitter's yomosaka and DasDokter, and eventually became the official representation of the fanart.

Shiori and Biboo Knock Knock

This is a short animation of Vtuber Group-Hololive Production, characters(vtuber talents) are Shiori Novella and Koseki Bijou. They were playing Minecraft when they played a fun little game called Knock Knock, which resulted in almost a minute of adorable interaction.

This animation was created using Mine-Imator and Modelbench.
